I am currently a Master student at Institute of Atmospheric Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, working with Prof. Lijing Cheng. My research focuses on understanding the ocean's role in a changing climate based on observation. My interests are wide-ranging and include, but are not limited to:

  • Earth's energy flow and water cycle,
  • Contemporary sea level budget,
  • Atlantic meridional heat and freshwater transport.
